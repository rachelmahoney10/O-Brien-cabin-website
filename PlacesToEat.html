Places to Eat

Salida

  1. Amicas-- Convivial pizzeria & microbrewery offering wood-fired pies & craft beer in bright, laid-back digs.
    2. http://www.amicassalida.com/
  2. Boathouse Cantina-- Vista-fueled restaurant serving Mexican fare & margaritas in casual riverfront surroundings.
    3. http://www.boathousesalida.com/
  3. Moonlight Pizza & Brewpub-- Modern-rustic joint with a foosball table & patio offering clever pizzas & house-brewed beer.
    4. http://www.moonlightpizza.biz/
  4. 50 Burger Shakes & Beer-- Everything is made in house with fresh, local ingredients.
    5. http://www.50burgersalida.com/
  5. Twisted Cork Cafe-- They strive to provide delicious food made with the highest quality ingredients.
    6. http://www.twistedcorkcafe.com/
  6. Elevation Beer Company-- A cool place to stop for a craft beer and something from the food truck.
    7. http://www.elevationbeerco.com/

Buena Vista

  1. Eddyline Resturant & Brewery-- Bustling microbrewery & restaurant featuring wood-fired meat dishes & pizza, plus handcrafted brews.
    2. http://eddylinerestaurant.com/food_menu/
  2. K's Dairy Delite-- Casual place you can stop for lunch or dinner and eat outside with a beautiful view of mountains surrounding you.
    3. https://www.facebook.com/Ks-Dairy-Delite-264983416594/
  3. House Rock Kitchen-- A lively patio with games abuts this modern, health-conscious stop for burgers, sandwiches & bowls.
    4. http://www.houserockkitchen.com/

Leadville

  1. Quincys Steak & Spirits-- Filet mignon & prime rib plus cocktails served in a comfortable, Western-inspired atmosphere.
    2. http://quincystavern.com/
  2. High Mountain Pies-- Laid-back eatery with a snug dining room & outdoor seating serving pizzas, calzones & sandwiches.
    3. http://highmtnpies.com/
  3. Periodic Brewing-- Periodic Brewing is a startup beer brewery in Leadville, Colorado that opened in the Fall of 2015.
    4. http://www.periodicbrewing.com/
